Our Creation Process

1. Concept & Inspiration

Initial discussions to capture your idea and the project's environment.

2. Digital Design

3D modeling and rendering of the mosaic within its architectural context.

3. Material Selection

Selection of tesserae (glass, stone, metal) and texture verification.

4. Color Mapping

Precise color mapping to ensure the fidelity of the final work.

5. Approval & Production

Final client validation before launching robotic production.
